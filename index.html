About Me

Hello, my name is Noah Holm and i'm a 2nd year Computer Science student at the University of Nottingham. I'll be spending my summer interning as a software engineer in London. My interests include Distributed Systems, Local LLMs, and Operating Systems. Beyond that, I have a massive obsession with automation; if a task in my life is repetitive or annoying, I will inevitably write a script to handle it for me. I have a deep passion and interest in technology and always love to talk about it, message me on linkedin or email me.

In my free time I love to play sports, my current sport of choice is Baseball after growing up playing it; I play for the University of Nottingham Thieves. I'm also an avid music listener and love going to concerts and festivals. Some of my favourite artists are Bladee, Chief Keef, Yung Lean,Kanye West and smokedope2016.
