Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2026



This website functions as a personal portfolio. It features a live visitor counter and an interactive map displaying approximate visitor locations. Transparency is important to me, so this policy outlines exactly what data is collected and how it is used.

1. Data Collection & Processing

  • IP Address: Your public network IP identifier is logged to manage the unique visitor counter.
  • Geographic Data: Your IP address is sent to a third-party geolocation API to estimate your approximate location (such as country, city, and broad latitude/longitude coordinates) for the live map pin display.
  • Timestamp: The precise date and time of your visit.

2. Purpose of Processing

This information is gathered strictly for analytical and educational purposes specifically, to power the public visual map and visitor tracker on this portfolio. This data is kept secure, is not used for commercial tracking, and is never sold or shared with third-party advertising companies.

3. Data Deletion & Your Rights

Because your raw IP address is stored alongside your location, you have the right to request its complete removal from my database at any time.

To request the deletion of your visitor record, please send an email with your public IP address to:

privacy@noahholm.com
